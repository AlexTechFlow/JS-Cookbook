Section 1

The Agency developed an integrated marketing campaign including a new website and professional referral package for a major GTA specialty (Endodontics) dentistry practice.

As part of the new responsive website, an SEO strategy was implemented to provide local visibility and top keyword ranking for main procedures.

A six-page referral package was developed around the tag line of “Excellence in the Art and Science of Endodontics” and promoted the practice’s leadership in the field.
